Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Level Six Fitness Gym, Legazpi City

Level Six Fitness Gym ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a member or visitor.

01 Information We Collect

When you book a membership or interact with our website, we may collect the following:

  • Personal identifiers — Full name, email address, and phone number submitted through our booking form.
  • Membership data — Your selected plan, preferred start date, and booking timestamp.
  • Usage data — General browsing behavior on our website (no cookies are set by our own scripts).
  • Communication data — Messages sent to us via Facebook Messenger through our page chat widget.

We do not collect payment information directly. All transactions are handled in person at the gym.

02 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to operate our gym and serve you as a member:

  • To confirm and process your membership booking
  • To contact you regarding your membership status, schedule changes, or promotions
  • To assign and manage your locker, PT sessions, or nutrition guide (as applicable)
  • To respond to inquiries submitted via our website or Facebook page

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

03 Data Storage

Booking submissions made through the website are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for session reference. We do not operate a cloud database through this website. Any data you submit is also received by us via email or our gym's management records.

Physical membership records are stored securely at the gym premises and accessible only to authorized staff.

04 Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Facebook Messenger Chat — Enables direct messaging through our Facebook Page. Messages are subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps Embed — Displays our location. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — Loads typography assets. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

05 Your Rights

As a data subject under Philippine law (Republic Act No. 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data from our records
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at our gym or via Facebook Messenger.

06 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at the gym or send a message through our Facebook Page.
