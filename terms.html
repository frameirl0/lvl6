Terms of Use

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Level Six Fitness Gym, Legazpi City

By accessing our website or becoming a member of Level Six Fitness Gym, you agree to these Terms of Use. Please read them carefully before proceeding.

01 Membership Eligibility

  • Members must be at least 15 years old. Those aged 15–17 require written parental or guardian consent.
  • You must be in reasonably good health to participate in gym activities. If you have a pre-existing medical condition, consult a physician before enrolling.
  • Level Six Fitness Gym reserves the right to refuse or revoke membership at its discretion if a member violates gym rules or these terms.

02 Gym Rules and Conduct

All members are expected to maintain a respectful and disciplined environment:

  • Re-rack weights and return equipment after every use.
  • Wear proper athletic footwear at all times on the gym floor.
  • Personal hygiene is required — bring a towel and wipe down equipment after use.
  • No aggressive behavior, harassment, or intimidation of staff or other members.
  • No photography or recording of other members without explicit consent.
  • Food and beverages (except water) are not permitted on the gym floor.
  • Members under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances will be denied entry and may have their membership revoked.

03 Health and Safety

  • Members participate in gym activities at their own risk. Level Six Fitness Gym is not liable for injuries resulting from improper use of equipment or failure to follow safety guidelines.
  • In the event of an injury, notify gym staff immediately. First aid is available on-site.
  • Members with medical conditions should inform their assigned coach before beginning any program.
  • Spotters are available upon request for heavy compound lifts.

04 Personal Training Sessions

  • PT sessions included in Premium plans must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Sessions that are cancelled with less than 12 hours notice or missed without prior notice will be forfeited for that billing cycle.
  • PT sessions are non-transferable and cannot be carried over to the following month unless otherwise agreed.

05 Locker Use

  • Assigned lockers must not be used to store valuables overnight unless you have a 24-hour lock-in arrangement.
  • The gym is not responsible for lost or stolen items left unattended.
  • Locker assignments may be reassigned upon membership cancellation or downgrade.

06 Website Use

  • This website is for informational and booking purposes only.
  • Submitting a booking form does not constitute a confirmed membership until you receive confirmation from gym staff.
  • You agree not to misuse, scrape, or attempt to disrupt the website or its functionality.

07 Amendments

Level Six Fitness Gym reserves the right to update these Terms of Use at any time. Members will be notified of significant changes. Continued use of the gym or website after such changes constitutes acceptance.
