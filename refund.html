Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Level Six Fitness Gym, Legazpi City

We want every member's experience at Level Six to be worth every peso. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds or credits may be issued.

01 General Policy

All membership fees are non-refundable once a billing cycle has begun and the member has accessed the gym. We encourage you to review our plans carefully before committing.

However, we handle refund requests on a case-by-case basis with fairness and transparency — see the exceptions below.

02 Eligible for Refund or Credit

  • Medical incapacity — If you are medically unable to continue using the gym (with supporting documentation from a licensed physician), a prorated credit or refund for unused days may be issued.
  • Gym closure — If Level Six Fitness Gym closes or suspends operations for reasons within our control, a full prorated refund for the affected period will be provided.
  • Booking error — If you were charged incorrectly due to a billing error on our part, a full correction will be made within 5 business days.
  • Pre-start cancellation — If you cancel your membership booking before your start date and have not used any gym services, a full refund of any upfront fee paid will be issued.

03 Not Eligible for Refund

  • Cancellations made after the membership start date and gym access has been used
  • Missed personal training sessions without prior cancellation notice (less than 12 hours)
  • Failure to use included services (e.g., unused PT sessions, nutrition guides)
  • Voluntary cancellation mid-cycle without qualifying circumstances
  • Membership revocation due to violation of gym rules or Terms of Use

04 Membership Pause

As an alternative to cancellation, members may request to pause their membership for up to 30 days per year in cases of:

  • Medical recovery (with documentation)
  • Travel or extended absence (minimum 2-week pause)

A membership pause freezes your billing cycle and reserves your slot. To request a pause, speak to gym staff in person or message us on Facebook.

05 How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund request:

  • Visit the gym in person and speak to the staff or manager on duty, or
  • Send a message to our Facebook Page with your full name, membership plan, and reason for the request

Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days through the same payment method used, or as cash depending on the original transaction.

06 Disputes

If you believe a refund was wrongly denied, you may escalate the matter to our gym owner/manager. We are committed to resolving disputes fairly and promptly. If no resolution is reached, the matter may be referred to the appropriate consumer protection authority under Philippine law.

Need to request a refund or pause? Visit us in person or message us through our Facebook Page. We'll respond within 24 hours.
