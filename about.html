About Level Six

Legazpi City, Albay  ·  Built on the 6S Framework

Level Six Fitness Gym was built for people who are serious about results. Not the kind that fade after a month, the kind that stick because they're built on structure, consistency, and a system that actually works.

01 Our Story

We started with one goal: give Legazpi City a gym that treats fitness like a discipline, not a trend. No gimmicks, no shortcuts. Just a well-equipped space, a committed coaching team, and a framework designed to move people forward regardless of where they're starting from.

Every program we run is built on the LVL VI 6S Framework, a system that combines Structure, Strength, Strategy, Sustainability, Support, and Self-Discipline into everything we do. It's not a slogan. It's the operating principle behind every plan, every session, and every result we've helped our members achieve.

02 Mission

To deliver structured, results-driven fitness programs that are accessible to every level of athlete from first-timers to seasoned lifters and to hold every member accountable to the progress they came here to make.

03 Vision

To be Albay's leading performance gym known not just for the equipment, but for the measurable transformations that happen inside these walls and continue long after members leave.

04 What We Stand For

  • Discipline over motivation. Motivation is temporary. We build habits that hold.
  • Structure over guesswork. Every program is intentional, periodized, and tracked.
  • Consistency over intensity. Showing up regularly beats burning out fast every time.
  • Transparency. No hidden fees, no overselling, no empty promises just honest coaching.
  • Community. A non-judgmental space where effort is respected at every level.
